Wave attenuation in vegetation based break water

Objective:

To understand the wave attenuation pattern in vegetation based wave flume.


Apparatus used:

Wave flume tank, Piles, Inverter, Pump


 Description Wave Attenuation is the change in wave transmission occurring when a wave passes through a permeable or porous medium.
